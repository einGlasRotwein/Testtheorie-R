The test was administed on one page as fourty paired
items. Users had to choose the one that fit them the
best.

(*) = key (endorsement is interpreted as narcissistic response)
F1, ..., F7 = factor affiliation as observed in Raskin & Terry (1988)

F1 = Authority        (Autoritätsgefühl)
F2 = Self-Sufficiency (Selbstgenügsamkeit)
F3 = Superiority      (Überlegenheitsgefühl)
F4 = Exhibitionism    (Prahlerei)
F5 = Exploitativeness (Ausbeutung)
F6 = Vanity           (Eitelkeit)
F7 = Entitlement      (Anspruchshaltung)


The following demographics were entered on the next
page. On this page they were also asked to affirm that
their answers were accurate and suitable for research,
those that did not (9%) are not included in this
dataset.

gender. Chosen from a drop down list (1=male, 2=female,
3=other; 0=none was chosen).

age. Entered as a free response. 


Items from:

Raskin, R., & Terry, H. (1988). A principal-components
analysis of the Narcissistic Personality Inventory and
further evidence of its construct validity. Journal of
personality and social psychology, 54(5), 890-902.
